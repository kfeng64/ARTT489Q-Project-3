About Us

We are the University of Maryland Club Climbing Team! Anyone is welcome, whether you have no idea what climbing is or you sleep under rocks so you don't have to get too far away from them. We compete in the Mid-Atlantic Collegiate Climbing Series (MACCS) and the Collegiate Climbing Series (CCS).
